Mr Muftic built “Discord MCPBot,” a free, browser-based app that lets anyone control Discord by voice—even people who struggle with keyboards. Just say, “Create a team meeting tomorrow at 3 pm” and the bot turns your words into the right Discord commands, handling time-zones and event details automatically.
