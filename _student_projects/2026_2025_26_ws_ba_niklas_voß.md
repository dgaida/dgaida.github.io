Mr Voß built a privacy-friendly AI study buddy that lets classmates chat with their own lecture notes and PDFs without sending data to the cloud. After testing 56 different set-ups, the best combination found answers twice as fast and more accurately than popular online tools. The open-source program is now ready for any university to copy and adapt for free.
